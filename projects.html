Color Sorter Machine

Built a conveyor-based system where materials are detected by a color sensor. Integrated Arduino with TCS3200 color sensor and actuators for decision-making. Used servo motors to separate objects into different bins depending on their color. Implemented real-time processing to ensure high accuracy and speed.

Regenerative Braking System

Designed for EVs, optimized with ML-based strategies to maximize energy recovery. Designed and implemented a regenerative braking system for electric vehicles to recover kinetic energy into battery storage. Improved vehicle range and energy efficiency through intelligent braking control strategies.
